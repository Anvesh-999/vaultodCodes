Chocolate Cake

⏱️ Prep: 45 mins
🍽️ Serves: 8
⌛ Timer: 00:00

Ingredients

  • 1 ¾ cups (220g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ cups (300g) granulated sugar
  • ¾ cup (75g) unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 ½ tsp baking powder
  • 1 ½ tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 large eggs, room temperature
  • 1 cup (240ml) milk
  • ½ cup (120ml) vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup (240ml) boiling water

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 175°C (350°F). Grease and line two 9-inch pans.
  2. Whisk dry ingredients: flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Beat in eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla until smooth.
  4. Carefully mix in boiling water (batter will be thin).
  5. Divide batter evenly into pans.
  6. Bake for 30–35 minutes, until a skewer comes out clean.
  7. Cool in pans 10 minutes, then transfer to a rack.
  8. Frost and decorate as you like.
